Your Zomato dataset in short:

* **Dataset:** Restaurant data from Zomato
* **Rows:** **9,551 restaurants**
* **Columns:** **17 features**
* **Contains information about:**

  * Restaurant name
  * City and country
  * Cuisines
  * Average cost for two
  * Ratings
  * Votes
  * Price range
  * Online delivery availability
  * Table booking availability

**Main purpose of analysis:**

* Find top cities with restaurants
* Identify popular cuisines
* Analyze ratings and customer behavior
* Study cost vs ratings
* Check impact of online delivery and table booking
* Discover business insights for restaurant growth

**Short business insight:**
Most restaurants do not offer online delivery, and ratings vary by city, creating opportunities to improve services and customer satisfaction.
